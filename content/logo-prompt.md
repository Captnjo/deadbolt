# Deadbolt Logo Prompt

## Prompt

Design a minimal, modern logo for "Deadbolt" — an open-source signing gateway for AI agents on Solana. The product sits between AI agents and a crypto wallet, requiring human approval before any transaction can be signed.

The logo should convey: security, physical control, and the boundary between autonomy and authority. Think of a deadbolt lock mechanism — the decisive, deliberate action of locking or unlocking.

Style: clean, geometric, monochrome (should work in black on white and white on black). No gradients. No illustration-heavy detail — this needs to read at 16px favicon size and on a CLI terminal. Think Vercel, Linear, or Warp-level logo simplicity.

Concept directions to explore:
- A stylized deadbolt latch in the locked position — the rectangular bolt extended
- A keyhole or lock face abstracted into geometric shapes
- The letter "D" integrated with a bolt or latch mechanism
- A shield or gate shape with a horizontal bar (the bolt) cutting through it
- A circuit-board-meets-lock aesthetic (bridging hardware and software)

Avoid: generic padlock shapes, blockchain/crypto cliches (no chains, no coins, no hexagons), overly complex illustrations, any text in the logomark itself (wordmark will be separate).

Deliver: a logomark (icon only, no text) and a lockup (logomark + "Deadbolt" wordmark in a clean sans-serif).

Color: primary black/white. Optional single accent color suggestion for brand use.
